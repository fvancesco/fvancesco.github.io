I am in the fouth year of my phd. I am interested in Language in Social Media, and its evolution. I studied figurative language in Twitter (irony, sarcasm and satire) and recently started to explore the visual aspect of social media, studying images and multimodal form of communication like emojis.

I received my bachelor's degree in Computer Science from the University of Bologna in December 2010 with final thesis on Quantum Computing (supervised by Ugo Dal Lago). Then I moved to Dublin (Ireland), where I worked as a videogame tester for Keywords Studios. After a year, I started a M.Sc. in Cognitive Science at University College Dublin. During my M.Sc. thesis (2013) I worked on irony detection in Twitter, supervised by Tony Veale.
I am currently a Ph.D. candidate at UPF (Barcelona), working on automatic detection of Irony, Sarcasm and Humour. My supervisor is Horacio Saggion.

From January to April 2016 I visited Marco Baroni's group at the University of Trento, and worked on Deeplearning Multimodal systems on Twitter.

Curently, I am working on multimodal systems at Snapchat (Aug'17 -> Dec'17).
